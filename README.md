 Lead Capture & Notification Automation (n8n)

 Overview

This workflow automates the process of capturing new leads, storing their information, and notifying the business owner in real time.

It eliminates manual data entry and ensures no lead is missed.

 Workflow Logic

Trigger: New lead submits a form
→ Add lead data to Google Sheets
→ Send email notification to founder

 Tools Used

- n8n (workflow automation)
- Google Sheets (lead database)
- Gmail (email notifications)

Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. Webhook Trigger

- Captures incoming form data (name, email, message)

2. Data Processing

- Formats and prepares lead information

3. Google Sheets Integration

- Automatically adds new lead as a row in the database

4. Email Notification

- Sends instant alert to business owner with lead details

Problem Solved

Before automation:

- Leads could be missed
- Manual data entry was required
- Slow response time

After automation:

- All leads are captured instantly
- Organized in a central database
- Founder gets notified immediately

 Impact

- Saves time on admin tasks
- Improves response speed
- Ensures consistent lead tracking
